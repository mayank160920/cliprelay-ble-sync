Join the Android Beta

ClipRelay for Android is currently in closed beta. Follow the two steps below to get early access and help us shape the app before public release.

1 Join the tester group

First, join our Google Group with the same Google account you use on your Android device. This registers you as a beta tester.

Join cliprelay-beta Google Group

2 Install the app

Once you've joined the group, open the link below to access ClipRelay on Google Play. It may take a few minutes for your tester status to activate after joining the group.

Install ClipRelay from Google Play

Note: You must use the same Google account for both steps. If the Play Store says the app isn't available, wait a few minutes and try again — it can take up to 15 minutes for Google to sync your tester status.

The Mac companion app is available as a direct download — no beta signup needed.
