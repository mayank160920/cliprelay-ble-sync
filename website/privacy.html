Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

ClipRelay transfers clipboard data exclusively over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), directly between your devices. No servers, no cloud, no internet connection required.

All transfers are fully end-to-end encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Encryption keys are generated during pairing and never leave your devices.

That's it. ClipRelay has no backend, so there is nothing to collect.
