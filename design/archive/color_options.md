# ClipRelay Color Options

## Current Choice: Neon Aqua

We're going with **Neon Aqua (#00FFD5)** as the primary color theme. It stands out in Android share menus (which are dominated by blues and grays), avoids the battery/power app association that neon green carries, and occupies a color lane that's largely unoccupied by other common apps.

## Options Evaluated

### 1. Neon Green
- **Hex:** #39FF14
- **Gradient:** #39FF14 → #00CC00
- **Pros:** Maximum visibility, high energy
- **Cons:** Strong battery/power app association (AccuBattery, Greenify, etc.); at small icon sizes users pattern-match on color before reading the silhouette

### 2. Neon Aqua (selected)
- **Hex:** #00FFD5
- **Gradient:** #00FFD5 → #00DDC0
- **Pros:** Distinctive in share menus, no wrong-category associations, conveys data transfer/connectivity
- **Cons:** None significant

### 3. Ice Cyan
- **Hex:** #00E5FF
- **Gradient:** #00E5FF → #00B8D4
- **Pros:** Clean, modern, tech-forward
- **Cons:** Too close to the blues that dominate Android share sheets (Messages, Drive, Chrome, Duo); would blend in rather than stand out
